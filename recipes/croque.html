Brian's Croque Monsieur

Ingredients

  1. Butter the 8 slices of sandwich bread on one side only.Place 1 slice of cheese on each slice of sandwich bread. Place 1 slice of ham, folded in half, on 4 slices of sandwich bread. Cover with the remaining slices of bread (unbuttered side up).
  2. In a bowl, mix the grated cheese with the milk, salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  3. Spread the mixture over the croque-monsieur.
  4. Place on a baking sheet under the broiler for 10 min.
  5. Serve!
